FuckOurOrdinaryLives

Making his debut in late 2009, Rasmus has taken his name to heart – Fuck Our Ordinary Lives – and risen to the top of the melodic electro scene, winning over thousands of fans worldwide. With massive support from the electronic music blogosphere ever since the free internet release of his debut EP Ashy Diamond Lungs (Late 2009), F.O.O.L has become a near-household name in the scene. Having played every other continent on the planet, F.O.O.L brings his unrelenting sound and notoriously high-energy show wherever he goes!
